Instructions for color stroop game
Hello!
It looks like you’ll be judging our game here today. What you’ll have to do is rather simple, each round the players will be compete in providing a series of 10 responses. They are shown a word which is both the name of a color and written in a colored font, their goal is to correctly identify the font color. You’ll be given a score sheets like the one at right. The winner should be whoever had the most correct responses, if there’s a tie you may select the winner based faster reaction times.
